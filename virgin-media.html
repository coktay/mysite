Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about switching to Virgin Media

How does the Virgin Media Refer a Friend program work? +

Virgin Media's Refer a Friend program allows existing customers to refer friends and family to Virgin Media services. The specific rewards offered may vary over time as Virgin Media updates their promotional offers. Both the referrer and the new customer typically receive some form of reward when a successful referral is completed and the new service is activated.

What Virgin Media packages qualify for referrals? +

Most Virgin Media broadband, TV, and phone bundles qualify for the referral program. This typically includes standalone broadband packages as well as combined bundles with TV and phone services. Mobile-only packages might have different terms. Check Virgin Media's current terms and conditions at the time of your application for the most up-to-date information.

How long does it take to get installed? +

Installation typically takes 1-2 weeks from placing your order, depending on your location and installation requirements. A Virgin Media technician will visit your home to set up the equipment and ensure everything is working properly. The installation process itself usually takes 1-2 hours.

When will I receive my referral rewards? +

According to Virgin Media's typical terms, any rewards from the Refer a Friend program are usually processed after the new customer's service has been successfully installed and activated. The exact timeframe can vary based on current promotions, but rewards are typically sent within a few weeks after installation. Always check the current terms on Virgin Media's website for the most accurate information.

Do I need a phone line for Virgin Media broadband? +

No, Virgin Media broadband doesn't require a traditional phone line. Unlike providers that use the BT Openreach network, Virgin has its own cable network. You can choose to add a phone service to your package if you want one, but it's not required for broadband service.

Can I keep my current phone number? +

Yes, you can keep your existing phone number when you switch to Virgin Media. During the sign-up process, you'll be asked if you want to transfer your current number. Virgin Media will handle the transfer process with your previous provider.

What happens when my contract ends? +

When your contract ends (typically after 18 months), your price will increase to the standard rate mentioned in your contract. Virgin Media will notify you before this happens, giving you the opportunity to negotiate a new deal or switch to a different package. You're free to cancel or switch providers without penalty once your minimum term is complete.
